E-registration.

In the existing system the registration take one month and above to be registered to RCA, now using your connected device, telephone, tablet or computer you can register a cooperative in 24 hours only.

E-joining

By browsing through the system member / people can find cooperatives which they can join and by meeting the rules and conditions required to join that cooperative they can register and join cooperatives they want and get approved or rejected in 24 hours.
